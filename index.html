1. Direct Custody Routing & Retail Netting (Scenario A & B)

DonKAI implements a hybrid clearinghouse architecture. For wagers exceeding one hundred thousand USDC, capital is routed directly into isolated child wallets in BitGo qualified custody. Retail orders under one hundred thousand USDC are matched internally on our order book, earning a risk-free staking yield of four point five percent APR, and only swept to the Susquehanna OTC desk once net pool exposure crosses the netting threshold.

2. Dynamic Black-Scholes Delta Hedging

To maintain risk neutrality, our broker engine continuously calculates option pricing and portfolio delta using a Black-Scholes volatility engine. When a directional imbalance is identified, the system programmatically routes hedging execution orders through Coinbase Prime and decentralized Jupiter swaps on Solana, protecting the platform's solvency.

3. Weighted Multi-Oracle Consensus & Arbitration

We resolve contracts using a weighted M-of-N consensus model that aggregates feeds from Chainlink, Pyth Network, and the Federal Reserve. If a dispute is filed within the twenty-four hour challenge window, wagers are held in escrow and escalated to an independent multi-sig committee, removing any single point of failure.

4. Cryptographic State Chain Audit Ledger

Every transaction block and EOD rebalancing sweep is recorded in an append-only cryptographic ledger. Utilizing SHA-256 hash chaining, each block mathematically locks to its predecessor. Any unauthorized attempt to inject database records breaks the hash chain instantly, automatically halting all BitGo disbursements.
